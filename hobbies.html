Ukuleles

I love ukuleles! It's very easy to pick up and they are very fun to play.

Here are some musicians that I try to imitate - 1, 2, 3.

Although the ukulele is a simple instrument with only 4 strings, the fingerpicking in 1, 2 and the body tapping in 3 show how flexible it is.

I own three ukuleles. My starter ukulele was a Makala Dolphin Soprano. I also have a Flight Concert which is what I play the most. My most recent addition is a Kala Tenor with a low G-string that has a nice thumpy bass sound when body tapping.
